# Data Consumption

The consumption layer provide access to the transformed data for business users and downstream systems.

## Consumption Channels

- **Looker**: Enterprise reporting and self-service BI.
- **Power BI**: Departmental reporting and visualization.
- **Ad-hoc Extracts**: Direct access to curated datasets for custom analysis.
- **Napier**: Regulatory reporting integration.
