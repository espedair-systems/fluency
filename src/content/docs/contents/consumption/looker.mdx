# Looker

Looker is our primary enterprise business intelligence and data visualization platform, enabling self-service analytics across the organization.

## Key Features

- **LookML**: A powerful modeling language for defining business metrics and relationships.
- **Explores**: Curated data views for non-technical users to build their own reports.
- **Dashboards**: Real-time visual monitoring of key business performance indicators.

## Access & Governance

- Access is managed through corporate SSO.
- Data models are version-controlled via GitHub.
- Standardized definitions ensure a single source of truth across all departments.
