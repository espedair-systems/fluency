# Business Consumption Layer

The Business Consumption Layer provides user-friendly, high-performance views of data for reporting and analysis.

## Key Components

- **Stars (Fact & Dimension Tables)**: Standard dimensional modeling for BI tools.
- **Views**: Virtualized layers for specific business domains.
- **Curated Datasets**: Pre-joined and aggregated data for self-service analysis.

## Principles

- **Ease of Use**: Optimized for business tools like Looker and Power BI.
- **Consistency**: Ensures all users see the same "truth" for business metrics.
- **Performance**: Structured for fast query response times.
